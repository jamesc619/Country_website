History

Historically, Canada was a British and French colony. On April 17, 1982, Canada gained full independece from Britain after 219 years of rule.

In 1534, French explorer Jacques Cartier explored the Gulf of Saint Lawrence where he planted a 10-meter (33 ft) cross bearing the words, "long live the King of France", and took possession of the territory New France in the name of King Francis I.

After the Seven Years War, Canada came under British Rule after 1763.
